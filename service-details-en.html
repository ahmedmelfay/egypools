Liner

The invention of the PVC liner the most significant thing that has ever happened in the global swimming pool industry.

Because the material remains flexible to absorb any slight movement of the pool shell and existing cracks for new or renovated swimming pools.

The 1.5mm PVC liners are suitable for every type of pool starting with private schools for fun and relaxation to Olympic pools for serious competition up to the heaviest used commercial pools and the pools with the highest environmental loading from sun and high temperature fluctuations to ice and snow.

Mosaic

Best suited for external and interior work EGYPools is the representative of Revista Mosaic (Turkey).

Revista manufacture glass mosaic tiles designed to be used in residentiel, pools cover and decorations, glass mosaic tile products are resistant to chemical attack do not absorb water and do not discolor in normal environmental conditions.

Tiles are manufactured by recycle clean glass and non recycled clear glass at 800 degree to meet or exceed the TSI standards

Water Slides

Fiberglass and Polyester water slides for Aqua parks, wave pools, and rafting rivers EGYPools is the One of the companies representative in egypt installation of fiberglass and polyester water slides for Aqua parks, wave pools, and rafting rivers.

It is the One of the companies that produces water slides with automatic machines and all procedures established and apply concerning quality management system for production, installation of waterslides for water parks and construction materials; are certified by TUV CERT Certification body of TUV Industry Service GmbH and ASTM.

ARTIFICAL ROCKS

Hand crafted in molds taken from real rocks and formations Rocks are made of inert polyethylene reinforce d concrete, approximately 3/4" thick. They are hand crafted in molds taken from real rocks and formations. These rocks are very strong, durable, and because of the concrete and the molding process they look very realistic.

EGY Pools teams will allow you to create natural environment that would look be either impossible or too expensive to do with real rocks. We offer a series of pre-engineered artificial waterfalls that can be combined with each other to create a beautiful natural environment.

Artificial rock allows pool owner to create natural environments that would either be impossible or too expensive to do with real rock. That's why so many builders are using artificial rock today as a "realistic" alternative to the limits of real rock construction. We offer a series of pre-engineered artificial waterfalls that can be combined with each other and our series of accent rocks to create a beautiful natural environment.

Filtration

Built-in filters have been used for more than thirty years around the world and have won the trust of many users thanks to their efficiency, reliability and simplicity. They are more and more replacing traditional sand filters that require a network of pipes buried underground and mechanical rooms that occupy spaces and need specialists in the maintenance of swimming pools, especially public ones.

We at EGY Pools believe in integrated filtration technology because it is environmentally friendly, and we have developed special filtration units with an international factory to meet the requirements of our region in Egypt and the Middle East.
